HDI Dimensions & Pillars

The Human Development Index is computed from three core dimensions measuring capability and welfare.

Long & Healthy Life

Measured by life expectancy at birth. Reflects health infrastructure, water, sanitation, and safety policies.

Knowledge Access

Measured by expected and mean years of schooling. A key indicator of social capital and human capability.

Decent Living Standard

Measured by GNI per capita (PPP in USD). Represents the economic capability of citizens.
